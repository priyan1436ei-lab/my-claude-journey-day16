# Day 16 – Stock Fundamental Research Custom Skill

## Objective

Create and test a reusable Claude Custom Skill named **stock-fundamental-research** for analyzing listed companies using fundamental analysis.

---

## Task Completed

### Skill Created

**Skill Name:** stock-fundamental-research

**Description:**
Analyze Indian and global listed companies using fundamentals, financial statements, business quality, competitive advantages, valuation, risks, and growth prospects. Generate evidence-based research reports and investor-friendly summaries. Never provide direct buy, sell, or hold recommendations.

## Key Learnings

### 1. Reusable Research Framework

The custom skill eliminates the need to repeatedly enter complex prompts.

### 2. Consistent Analysis

The skill follows a standardized structure across different companies.

### 3. Better Research Quality

Fundamental metrics such as ROE, ROCE, Debt-to-Equity, Free Cash Flow, and Valuation Ratios are analyzed consistently.

### 4. Risk Awareness

The skill highlights governance concerns, leverage risks, and industry headwinds.

### 5. Investor-Friendly Reports

Complex financial concepts are simplified into easy-to-understand summaries.
